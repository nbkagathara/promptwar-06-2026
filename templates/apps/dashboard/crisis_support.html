Instant Grounding: The 5-4-3-2-1 Technique

If you are experiencing a panic attack or high anxiety, follow this sensory mindfulness technique to bring your focus back to the present moment:

5 Sight

Look around and name 5 things you can see in the room right now.

4 Touch

Identify 4 things you can physically feel (e.g. chair support, clothes, pen).

3 Sound

Listen and name 3 distinct sounds you can hear (e.g. fan, traffic, voices).

2 Smell

Name 2 things you can smell (e.g. coffee, paper, breeze).

1 Taste

Focus on 1 thing you can taste (e.g. drinking a sip of water).
